## Step 2: U2U and Search: Assess the risk of harm

### Additional characteristics and existing controls

Some definitions:

Absence of Harmful Content
> Since our start of operations in 2022, there have been 6 reports by
> our users. None of these reports were about harmful content posted by
> any of our users. We also did not have to remove any content posted by
> our users.

Active Moderation Team
> Our moderation team is 18 strong, and separated over a diverse group
> of countries spanning 9 time zones. Most valid user generated reports
> are dealt with within a few minutes. Our moderation team also follows
> user posted content as far as possible. No harmful content was
> identified that way either.

Code of Conduct
> We apply the [Kubernetes Community Code of Conduct](https://kubernetes.io/community/code-of-conduct/) to all interactions on our service.

No Child Users
> We have no evidence of children posting or visiting our service in any
> significant numbers. Our Server Rules also explicitly say that the
> service might not be used for people under the age of 18.

Reporting Features
> Our software uses industry standard reporting functions which are
> easily accessible from within the service, as well as through third
> party clients. We also provide a direct email address in addition to
> the in-service feature on our Code of Conduct page.
